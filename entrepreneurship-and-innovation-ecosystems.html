Entrepreneurship & Innovation Ecosystems

Entrepreneurship & Innovation Ecosystems

The Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center (PERTC) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant entrepreneurship and innovation ecosystem in Pakistan. Our research and programs focus on supporting startups, scaling businesses, and enabling innovative solutions to economic and social challenges.

PERTC collaborates with entrepreneurs, investors, academia, and policymakers to identify barriers to innovation, develop effective support mechanisms, and promote an ecosystem conducive to sustainable business growth.

By integrating market insights, policy analysis, and international best practices, PERTC helps cultivate an environment where innovative ideas can thrive and create meaningful impact.

Innovation Assessment & Ecosystem Insights

PERTC emphasizes data-driven analysis of entrepreneurship trends and innovation dynamics. Our work identifies opportunities, challenges, and emerging sectors where entrepreneurial activity can drive economic growth and job creation.

Through careful evaluation of policies, market conditions, and institutional support mechanisms, PERTC provides actionable insights to strengthen startup ecosystems, access to finance, mentorship programs, and regulatory frameworks.

This approach enables entrepreneurs, investors, and policymakers to collaborate effectively, mitigate risks, and scale innovative solutions that contribute to sustainable economic development.

Strategic Approach & Support Mechanisms

PERTC applies a holistic approach to entrepreneurship and innovation, combining market research, policy evaluation, and ecosystem development strategies. This ensures interventions are impactful, scalable, and tailored to Pakistan’s entrepreneurial landscape.

Each initiative is designed to support founders, accelerate startups, and foster collaborative networks that encourage innovation and value creation.

Startup Support

Providing guidance, mentorship, and resources to help startups succeed in competitive markets.

Innovation Hubs

Facilitating networks, incubators, and co-working spaces to nurture creative solutions.

Policy & Regulatory Guidance

Advising on policies, incentives, and regulatory frameworks that enable entrepreneurship.

Programs & Ecosystem Engagement

PERTC ensures that research and initiatives in entrepreneurship translate into tangible ecosystem outcomes. Our outputs include startup frameworks, innovation roadmaps, advisory reports, and engagement with investors and policymakers.

Beyond publications, PERTC organizes workshops, networking events, and accelerator programs that strengthen entrepreneurial skills, encourage collaboration, and facilitate access to funding.

By fostering sustainable partnerships, promoting knowledge sharing, and evaluating ecosystem performance, PERTC contributes to a thriving innovation-driven economy in Pakistan.
