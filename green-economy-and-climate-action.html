Green Economy & Climate Action

Green Economy & Climate Action

The Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center (PERTC) is committed to promoting a sustainable green economy and advancing climate action initiatives. Our research focuses on identifying pathways for low-carbon growth, renewable energy adoption, and climate-resilient development strategies.

PERTC collaborates with policymakers, businesses, and civil society to analyze environmental challenges, evaluate green policies, and design solutions that balance economic growth with ecological sustainability.

By integrating environmental science, economic modeling, and policy insights, PERTC contributes to informed decision-making that addresses climate change while promoting sustainable development in Pakistan.

Environmental Assessment & Policy Insights

PERTC conducts comprehensive analysis of climate risks, environmental policies, and sustainable development initiatives. Our research evaluates renewable energy potential, carbon reduction strategies, and climate resilience measures.

By assessing the effectiveness of current policies and identifying gaps, PERTC provides actionable insights for designing incentives, regulations, and programs that encourage green investment and sustainable business practices.

This approach enables governments, businesses, and communities to adopt climate-smart solutions, anticipate environmental challenges, and integrate sustainability into economic planning.

Research Methodology & Sustainable Approach

PERTC applies an integrated methodology combining environmental economics, policy analysis, and impact assessment to ensure research is both rigorous and actionable.

Each study aims to identify sustainable growth opportunities, reduce environmental risks, and support policies that promote climate resilience and ecological balance.

Green Policy Evaluation

Systematic assessment of environmental policies and climate action programs for effectiveness and impact.

Renewable Energy & Efficiency

Analysis of renewable energy adoption, energy efficiency, and sustainable resource management.

Climate Risk & Resilience

Identifying climate risks and designing strategies to enhance societal and economic resilience.

Programs & Climate Engagement

PERTC ensures that research on green economy and climate action translates into meaningful impact. Our outputs include policy briefs, climate action roadmaps, advisory reports, and engagement with government and private sector stakeholders.

Beyond research, PERTC organizes workshops, consultations, and training programs to strengthen institutional capacity, promote sustainable practices, and enhance environmental awareness across sectors.

By fostering partnerships, monitoring progress, and evaluating outcomes, PERTC contributes to a sustainable, climate-resilient, and green economic future for Pakistan.
