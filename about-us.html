Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre - Driving Economic Revolution Through Knowledge, Skills, and Innovation

Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre

Nurturing Minds. Shaping Constructive Thoughts. Building the Nation.

PERTC is Pakistan's pioneer Research & Training institution, dedicated to driving economic revolution through knowledge, skills, and innovation. We operate at the intersection of research, policy, industry, and education.

We are committed to nurturing minds toward positive values, research & analytical thinking, innovation & technology adoption, entrepreneurship & enterprise creation, AI/AGI & Emerging Technologies, and Green Economy initiatives.

Our Philosophy

Economic transformation begins with people, mindset, and skills. Nations rise when minds are nurtured, thoughts are reshaped constructively, and citizens are empowered with knowledge, skills, and purpose.

Global Trade & Digital Transformation

Redefining Pakistan's position in the global digital economy

E-Commerce: The New Global Trade Engine

E-Commerce is now the world's most powerful economic engine and largest driver of global trade.

PERTC is designing one of the world's most advanced E-Business Platforms to:

  • Empower individuals as rational, informed consumers
  • Enable industries to access global markets seamlessly
  • Connect producers directly with consumers worldwide
  • Support SMEs, startups, and exporters
  • Drive inclusive digital economic growth

This platform will redefine how Pakistan trades with the world and position our nation as a leader in digital commerce.

Our Ultimate Objective

2047 Target Year

To position Pakistan among the Top Ten Economies of the World by 2047

Through a comprehensive strategy built on five foundational pillars that will transform Pakistan's economic landscape and global standing.

Knowledge-Based Economic Growth

Transforming Pakistan into an innovation-driven economy where research, technology, and intellectual capital become the primary drivers of growth and competitiveness.

01

Skilled Human Capital

Developing a future-ready workforce equipped with global-standard skills, digital literacy, and adaptive capabilities to excel in the evolving world economy.

02

Research-Driven Innovation

Establishing world-class research ecosystems that foster breakthrough innovations, technological advancements, and evidence-based policy formulation.

03

Sustainable & Green Development

Integrating environmental sustainability into economic planning, promoting green technologies, and building climate-resilient infrastructure for long-term prosperity.

04

Global Digital Trade Leadership

Positioning Pakistan as a leader in digital commerce, e-services exports, and technology-driven trade through advanced digital infrastructure and policies.

05

Expected Economic Impact

By achieving these five pillars, Pakistan will transform into a $3 trillion+ economy with per capita income exceeding $15,000, creating 50 million new jobs and becoming a global hub for technology, innovation, and sustainable development.
