Chairman's Message

Welcome to PERTC – Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre. Pakistan stands at a defining crossroads where the future of the nation will be shaped not by resources alone, but by the strength of research, innovation, skills, and visionary thinking. In today’s rapidly transforming world, economies rise on the foundation of knowledge, technology, and human capital. At PERTC, we firmly believe that the true power of Pakistan lies in the minds of its people.

Our vision is ambitious yet purposeful: to play a decisive role in transforming Pakistan into a knowledge-based, research-oriented, and skilled economy, capable of being counted among the top ten economies of the world by 2047. Achieving this goal requires a fundamental shift in national mindset — from imitation to innovation, from dependency to entrepreneurship, and from short-term thinking to long-term strategic planning.

PERTC has been established as a national center of excellence for research, training, and future-skills development. We are committed to nurturing minds toward research, entrepreneurship, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), emerging technologies, digital trade, and green economic initiatives. Our work is guided by global benchmarks, aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals, and focused on real, measurable national impact.

In an era defined by disruption and rapid change, continuous learning, adaptability, and innovation are no longer optional — they are essential. Through rigorous research, advanced training, and strategic collaboration with national and international partners, PERTC aims to empower youth, professionals, institutions, and industries to compete, lead, and excel in the global economy.

Our belief is clear and unwavering:
“Badal Do Soch, Badal Do Pakistan.”
When we transform thinking, we transform systems. When systems evolve, economies rise. And when economies strengthen, nations secure a prosperous, dignified, and sustainable future.

I invite policymakers, educators, researchers, industry leaders, and global partners to join PERTC in this mission of national transformation through knowledge, skills, and innovation. Together, let us nurture minds, shape constructive thoughts, and build the nation.

Prof. Nadeem Ahmed Faraz

Chairman, Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre (PERTC)
