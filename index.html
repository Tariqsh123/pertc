Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre

Nurturing Minds. Shaping Constructive Thoughts. Building the Nation.

PERTC is Pakistan's pioneer Research & Training institution, dedicated to driving economic revolution through knowledge, skills, and innovation. We operate at the intersection of research, policy, industry, and education.

We are committed to nurturing minds toward positive values, research & analytical thinking, innovation & technology adoption, entrepreneurship & enterprise creation, AI/AGI & Emerging Technologies, and Green Economy initiatives.

Our Philosophy

Economic transformation begins with people, mindset, and skills. Nations rise when minds are nurtured, thoughts are reshaped constructively, and citizens are empowered with knowledge, skills, and purpose.

Our Vision

To transform Pakistan into a Knowledge-Driven, Research-Oriented, Skilled, and Globally Competitive Economy, positioning our motherland among the Top Ten Economies of the World by 2047.

Our Mission

To transform the thinking of the nation through continuous, future-focused Research, Training, Innovation, and Capacity Building, fostering a culture of:

  • Research & Evidence-Based Decision Making
  • Innovation & Entrepreneurship
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) & AGI
  • Emerging & Disruptive Technologies
  • Green Economy & Sustainable Development

SDGs as Our Core Strategy

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) form the backbone of PERTC's strategy. Our roadmap is designed to achieve inclusive, resilient, and sustainable growth aligned with SDG-4 (Quality Education), SDG-8 (Decent Work), SDG-9 (Innovation), SDG-13 (Climate Action), and SDG-17 (Partnerships).

Global Trade & Digital Transformation

Redefining Pakistan's position in the global digital economy

E-Commerce: The New Global Trade Engine

E-Commerce is now the world's most powerful economic engine and largest driver of global trade.

PERTC is designing one of the world's most advanced E-Business Platforms to:

  • Empower individuals as rational, informed consumers
  • Enable industries to access global markets seamlessly
  • Connect producers directly with consumers worldwide
  • Support SMEs, startups, and exporters
  • Drive inclusive digital economic growth

This platform will redefine how Pakistan trades with the world and position our nation as a leader in digital commerce.
