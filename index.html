About PERTC

Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center

The Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center (PERTC) is a leading independent think tank dedicated to advancing evidence-based economic policy and capacity building in Pakistan.

Our institution brings together the nation's top economists, researchers, and policy experts to address Pakistan's most pressing economic challenges and opportunities.
