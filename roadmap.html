Roadmap

Strategic Roadmap to 2047

Our phased approach to transform Pakistan into a top 10 global economy

Phase I 2025–2030

Foundation & Capacity Building

Objective: Establish the foundation of a knowledge and skills-based economy

  • National economic & sectoral research programs
  • Future skills & digital workforce training
  • AI, data science & digital literacy initiatives
  • Entrepreneurship & startup incubation
  • Youth and women economic empowerment
Impact: A research-oriented, skill-enabled, and innovation-ready workforce.
Phase II 2030–2037

Innovation & Global Integration

Objective: Shift from skill adoption to innovation leadership

  • Advanced AI, AGI & emerging technology centres
  • Export-oriented entrepreneurship & SMEs
  • Digital trade and e-commerce ecosystems
  • Green economy & climate-smart enterprises
  • Industry–academia–government collaboration
Impact: Pakistan emerges as a regional innovation and digital trade hub.
Phase III 2037–2047

Global Economic Leadership

Objective: Achieve global competitiveness and economic leadership

  • Knowledge-based exports and services dominance
  • Global digital trade leadership
  • High-value technology and research industries
  • Circular and green economy integration
  • Strategic global partnerships
Impact: Pakistan secures its position among the Top Ten Global Economies.

SDGs as Our Core Strategy

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) form the backbone of PERTC's strategy. Our roadmap is designed to achieve inclusive, resilient, and sustainable growth aligned with SDG-4 (Quality Education), SDG-8 (Decent Work), SDG-9 (Innovation), SDG-13 (Climate Action), and SDG-17 (Partnerships).
