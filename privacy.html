Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Pakistan Economic Research & Training Centre (PERTC) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact number, and other details you voluntarily provide while interacting with our website, including newsletter subscriptions, contact forms, and event registrations.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to improve our services, respond to inquiries, provide updates, and deliver relevant content. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. PERTC is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by PERTC. You may also withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@pertc.org.pk.

8. Changes to This Policy

PERTC may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to review this page regularly for any changes. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
