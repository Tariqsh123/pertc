Future Skills & Workforce Development

Future Skills & Workforce Development

The Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center (PERTC) is committed to fostering the development of future-ready skills across the national workforce. Our programs and research focus on identifying critical skills gaps and promoting innovative training solutions to equip individuals for emerging economic sectors.

PERTC collaborates with educational institutions, industries, and policymakers to ensure that workforce development aligns with Pakistan’s evolving labor market demands and global competitiveness standards.

By integrating skill development frameworks with real-world labor market trends, PERTC supports informed policy decisions and effective programs that prepare citizens for tomorrow’s economy.

Skills Assessment & Workforce Planning

PERTC emphasizes evidence-based assessment of workforce skills and capacity. Our analysis identifies critical skill shortages, emerging job profiles, and sectoral trends to guide national training and employment strategies.

Through comprehensive evaluation, PERTC provides actionable insights for designing vocational programs, upskilling initiatives, and policy frameworks that meet the demands of a technology-driven, knowledge-intensive economy.

This approach enables educational institutions, government agencies, and employers to collaborate effectively, anticipate labor market shifts, and implement practical workforce solutions.

Training Methodology & Strategic Approach

PERTC applies a multi-dimensional approach to workforce development, combining skills gap analysis, competency-based training, and innovative learning technologies. This ensures programs are relevant, practical, and scalable across sectors.

Each initiative is designed to build employable skills, strengthen institutional capacity, and empower individuals to thrive in a rapidly changing economy.

Competency Mapping

Identification of critical skills required in key industries and future job roles.

Upskilling & Reskilling

Development of targeted training programs to upgrade existing workforce capabilities.

Industry Alignment

Collaboration with businesses and institutions to ensure training meets labor market needs.

Programs & Stakeholder Engagement

PERTC ensures that training initiatives translate into measurable outcomes. Our outputs include skill development frameworks, training modules, assessment tools, and policy recommendations for education and labor authorities.

Beyond program design, PERTC actively engages with industry leaders, educational institutions, and government agencies through workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects to enhance workforce readiness.

By fostering sustainable partnerships and continuous evaluation, PERTC contributes to a workforce capable of meeting Pakistan’s future economic challenges and opportunities.
