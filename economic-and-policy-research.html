Economic & Policy Research

Economic & Policy Research

The Pakistan Economic Research & Training Center (PERTC) is an independent think tank dedicated to advancing rigorous and policy- relevant economic research. Our work addresses Pakistan’s structural, institutional, and developmental challenges through objective analysis and empirical evidence.

PERTC’s research agenda is designed to support policymakers, public institutions, development partners, academics, and practitioners by providing reliable insights into macroeconomic management, governance reforms, and long-term growth strategies.

By integrating economic theory with real-world data and comparative international experience, PERTC contributes to informed decision- making and constructive national policy dialogue.

Policy Analysis & Economic Reform

PERTC places strong emphasis on policy-oriented economic analysis that directly informs national reform efforts. Our research examines fiscal sustainability, monetary policy effectiveness, public debt dynamics, taxation systems, and institutional governance.

By critically evaluating existing policies and reform initiatives, PERTC assesses their effectiveness, distributional impact, and long- term sustainability. Our work also incorporates political economy and administrative capacity considerations that shape policy outcomes.

This approach enables policymakers to better understand trade-offs, anticipate implementation challenges, and design pragmatic, evidence- based economic reforms aligned with Pakistan’s institutional realities.

Research Methodology & Analytical Approach

PERTC adopts a multidisciplinary research methodology combining quantitative economic modeling, qualitative institutional analysis, and comparative policy evaluation. This ensures analytical rigor and contextual relevance across all research initiatives.

Each study is designed to assess policy effectiveness, identify structural constraints, and propose feasible reform options within Pakistan’s socio-economic and governance framework.

Data-Driven Research

Utilization of national and international datasets to ensure empirical credibility and robust analysis.

Policy Evaluation

Systematic assessment of existing policies to measure impact, efficiency, and sustainability.

Institutional Context

Recognition of governance structures and political economy constraints shaping policy outcomes.

Research Outputs & Policy Engagement

PERTC ensures that research findings translate into meaningful policy engagement. Our outputs include policy briefs, technical reports, background papers, and advisory notes tailored for decision-makers at federal and provincial levels.

Beyond publications, PERTC engages stakeholders through structured consultations, workshops, and training programs aimed at enhancing institutional capacity and analytical skills within the public sector.

Through sustained collaboration with government institutions, universities, and international organizations, PERTC contributes to informed policy dialogue and supports Pakistan’s pursuit of inclusive and sustainable economic development.
